# tigil-gastos

TigilGastos is a web-based savings tracker that helps students save money for tuition by introducing a locking mechanism. Once a user sets a savings goal and activates the lock, they are restricted from withdrawing their savings until they reach their goal or the target date. This system promotes discipline by preventing impulsive spending while still being simple enough to implement. It provides users with a clear view of their progress through a visual interface such as a progress bar.

# PROBLEM: A college student in Quezon City struggles to consistently save for tuition because they frequently spend their money on daily expenses, leading to insufficient funds and stress when payment deadlines arrive.

# SOLUTION: TigilGastos allows the student to lock their savings through a Soroban smart contract, where deposits are recorded on-chain and cannot be withdrawn until the goal amount or date is reached, leveraging Stellar’s low fees and fast transactions to ensure secure and reliable savings enforcement.
